How It Works

Deploy. Patrol. Protect.

From installation to full autonomous coverage in three steps.

01
Map the Site
The SYRI unit walks your facility once, building a complete 3D map. No pre-programming, no manual waypoints. It learns the terrain on its own.
02
Learn the Baseline
It observes normal patterns — foot traffic, thermal signatures, vehicle movement — and establishes what "normal" looks like for your specific environment.
03
Detect & Alert
When something deviates, SYRI alerts your security team instantly with location, visuals, and threat classification — all without human intervention.
